Video Requirements¶

  1. Description

    1. At least 1 sentence on what the video is about

    2. Timecodes (if needed)

    3. All sources used in the video, including their respective authors

    4. Only 3 hashtags related to the video

      • Hashtags must be relevant

      • Hashtags must be different

      • Hashtag set must be mix of well known and niche

      • Re-use hashtags across similar videos, especially if they share a playlist.

    5. No unnecessary keywords

  2. High-definition thumbnails

  3. Associated playlist(s)

  4. Only 3 to 5 tags

    • Tags should be lower-case

    • Tags should be left-to-right, from least-to-most specific

    • Re-use hashtags across similar videos, especially if they share a playlist.

  5. Appropriate category

  6. Scheduled at 12:00am before the following..

    • Weekends (Friday and Saturday)
